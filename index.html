Tamay Besiroglu


tamay.besiroglu at gmail.com

I am an interdisciplinary AI researcher studying the progress, impacts, and responsible development of artificial intelligence. My research combines perspectives from computer science, economics, public policy, and forecasting to analyze AI trends and their implications for society. I'm a member of technical staff at MIT CSAIL. I'm also the Associate Director of Epoch. I hold an MPhil in Economics from the University of Cambridge, where I received the Stevenson Prize for top academic performance..
