I work on the intersection of economics and computing, focusing on the technical and economic basis for AI automation.

I'm the Associate Director of Epoch, and research affiliate at MIT CSAIL. I further advise a few groups, such as the Center for Space Governance, the forecasting technology platform Metaculus, and MIT AI Alignment (MAIA).

Research

My research focuses on the economics of computing, AI automation, and big-picture trends in machine learning.
