I'm the Associate Director of Epoch, and research affiliate at MIT CSAIL. I work on the intersection of economics and computing, focusing on the technical and economic basis for AI automation.

Research

My primary research focus delves deep into the synergy of the technical and economic frameworks underpinning AI automation. My insights bridge the divide between two vital domains, shaping the future of AI-driven economic models. Reach out to me to explore collaborative opportunities.
